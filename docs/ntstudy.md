### Codex Sinaiticus
~4th Century handwritten near complete copy of the New Testament.

The website hosting it has an excellent search and comparison system.

### The Oxyrhynchus Papyri
Volumes of ancient manuscripts found near Oxyrynchus, Egypt.

Includes New Testament stuff, but there's loads of other manuscripts that help build mental context for what people thought and did thousands of years ago.

Upenn.edu has volumes 1-16 free, pointing to their [Internet Archive links](https://onlinebooks.library.upenn.edu/webbin/serial?id=ocyrhynchus).
